Pizza

Description

Dough with sliced sausages and cheese... Do you still want more?

No problem

You can add tomato sauce, tomatoes itself, mushrooms, pepper. Everything you want!

Ingredients

  • Dough
  • Sausages
  • Cheese
  • Tomato sauce
  • Tomato
  • Pepper (optionally)
  • Mushtooms (optionally)

Steps

  1. Make a big dough tortilla
  2. Add the tomato sauce
  3. Add the slices sausages
  4. Add the mushrooms
  5. Grate and add the cheese
  6. Bake as much as you need

Done.
