Hamburger

Description

Bun, salad, meat, ketchup, cheese, slice of a fresh tomato. That's literally ALL you need to eat to crush your enemies with bare hands.

Even Gordon Ramzi himself eats hamburgers

You got this man

Ingredients

  • Bun
  • Salad
  • Meat
  • Cheese
  • Tomato
  • Ketchup

Steps

  1. Put the bun on the table
  2. Add the tomato
  3. Add the cheese
  4. Add the meat
  5. Add the ketchup
  6. Add the salad
  7. Cover everything with an another bun

Done.
